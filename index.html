Liangli Zhen

Senior Scientist at A*STAR

Welcome to my homepage! I am a group leader and senior scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing, Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore, and a Senior Member of the IEEE. I lead the AI Safety Research Group focused on enhancing AI safety and robustness for real-world deployment and application. Prior to joining A*STAR, I received my PhD in Computer Science from Sichuan University, China, supervised by Professor Dezhong Peng. I was a joint PhD student at The University of Birmingham in the UK, supervised by Professor Xin Yao.

My research is mainly on machine learning and optimisation. Related research topics include adversarial attacks, domain generalisation, jailbreak attacks, hallucination mitigation, multimodal learning, multi-objective optimisation, and more.
