Welcome to my homepage. I am a Research Scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. I am also a Principal Investigator and the leader of the Efficient AI team at IHPC, and we are devoted to improving data efficiency and computation efficiency in machine learning. Prior to joining IHPC, I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Sichuan University in 2018, supervised by Professor Dezhong Peng. From August 2016 to August 2018, I was a visiting PhD student at the University of Birmingham in the UK, supervised by Professor Xin Yao.

My research interests are in machine learning and multiobjective optimisation. Related research topics include representation learning, multimodal learning, deep neural networks, subspace clustering, cross-modal retrieval, blind source separation, many-objective evolutionary algorithms, etc.
